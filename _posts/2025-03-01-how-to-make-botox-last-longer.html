Getting Botox is an investment in your self-confidence. Naturally, you want it to last as long as possible. While Botox typically lasts 3-4 months, these tips can help you extend its effects:

1. Follow Your Post-Treatment Instructions
Avoid rubbing or massaging the treated area for at least 24 hours. Don't lie down or exercise for 4 hours after your appointment. These guidelines help prevent Botox from migrating to unintended areas.

2. Maintain a Consistent Treatment Schedule
Sticking to a regular Botox schedule (every 3-4 months) can help “train” the muscles to relax more consistently. Over time, you may even need fewer units.

3. Use Medical-Grade Skincare
Support your Botox results with products that boost collagen, hydration, and cellular repair. At Simply You, Nurse Aesthetics, we offer a curated selection of skincare to help you get the most out of your treatment.

Bonus Tip: Stay out of the sun, avoid smoking, and maintain a healthy lifestyle. These habits protect your skin and enhance your results.
