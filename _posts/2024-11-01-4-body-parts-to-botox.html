No more crows feet, smile lines, or wrinkled foreheads, BUT ALSO no more headaches, migraines, TMJ, or excessive sweating?!

That's right! Botox isn't just for pretty faces, it also has a wide range of medical applications.

Some patients that suffer from chronic headaches / migraines can find relief from botox. Although there are many causes of headaches / migraines, the condition can be irritated by tense face, head, and neck muscles. Botox can be injected around the head and neck to reduce headaches and migraines aggravated by muscle tightness.

TMJ (temporomandibular disorder) and jaw tension is often “treated” with a mouth guard. The mouth guard prevents teeth grinding but offers little to prevent the jaw clenching that causes pain. With Botox, the muscles around the jaw can reduce muscle activation resulting in less pain and less teeth grinding. No more jaw pain, botox for the win! `

Constantly dropping things because you have sweaty paws? Reapplying anti-perspirant every 30 mins but still soaking through your t-shirt? Botox can help. Patients with excessive sweating (hyperhidrosis) have over active sweat glands. Botox can be injected into the overactive glands to reduce and control perspiration. Once treated, say goodbye to having to carry an extra blouse to work every day.

Although Botox can help with these (and more) medical conditions it is important to speak with your primary care doctor (or your specialist) to ensure any underlying medical conditions are treated.
