The future of beauty is regenerative. Rather than just masking signs of aging, regenerative aesthetics help your body restore itself using its own natural processes.

What Is Regenerative Aesthetic Medicine?
It’s an emerging field that stimulates collagen, elastin, and tissue repair. Think of it as rejuvenation from the inside out—with longer-lasting, natural-looking results.

Popular Treatments at Simply You:

1. PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma)
PRP uses your body's own platelets to accelerate healing. Commonly known as the "vampire facial," it stimulates collagen, tightens skin, and improves texture—especially when combined with microneedling.

2. Microneedling
Microneedling creates controlled micro-injuries in the skin to boost natural healing. It reduces fine lines, acne scars, and uneven tone. Add PRP to boost results.

3. Sculptra®
Unlike hyaluronic fillers, Sculptra stimulates your body to produce collagen over time. Results appear gradually and last up to two years, restoring volume in a soft, natural way.

Is Regenerative Aesthetics Right for You?
If you're looking for subtle, long-lasting results that enhance your natural glow, regenerative treatments could be the perfect fit.
