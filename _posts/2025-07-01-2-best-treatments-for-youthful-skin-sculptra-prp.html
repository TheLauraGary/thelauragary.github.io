What Is Regenerative Aesthetic Medicine?
It's an emerging field that focuses on treatments that collagen, elastin, and tissue repair. Think of it as rejuvenation from the inside out—with longer-lasting, natural-looking results. Rather than just masking signs of aging, regenerative aesthetics help your body restore itself using its own natural processes.

At Simply You, Nurse Aesthetics we specialize in regenerative aesthetics and have found the 2 best treatments for more youthful skin are: PRP and Sculptra.

PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma)
PRP uses your body's own platelets to accelerate healing. Commonly known as the "vampire facial," it stimulates collagen, tightens skin, and improves texture—especially when combined with microneedling.

Sculptra®
Unlike hyaluronic fillers, Sculptra stimulates your body to produce collagen over time.

Results appear gradually and last up to two years, restoring volume in a soft, natural way.
