Laura Gary


Laura has over 17 years of experience in healthcare, including aesthetics, critical care, nursing education, and leadership. Known for her clinical expertise, personalized approach, and genuine care, she is dedicated to helping clients look and feel their best while staying true to who they are.

Laura holds a Bachelor of Science in Nursing from the University of Minnesota and a Master's degree from Norwich University. She is currently pursuing a second Master's degree to become a Family Nurse Practitioner, further expanding her ability to provide comprehensive, patient-centered care. Throughout her career, she has worked at leading medical centers and trained with top aesthetic professionals across the country. Her depth of knowledge, skill, and commitment to excellence sets her apart in the field of aesthetic medicine.

She believes in a thoughtful, evidence-based approach to treatments, one that respects individual goals, prioritizes safety, and delivers natural-looking results. Laura is passionate about continued education and stays at the forefront of advances in technique, products, and patient care.

Beyond Simply You, Laura is married to her high school sweetheart and a proud mom to three incredible children. Her life is rooted in family, purpose, and a deep desire to empower others through confidence and self-care.

She can't wait to welcome you to Simply You, where her mission is to help you feel refreshed, confident, and the best version of yourself.
