Fine lines and wrinkles tell the story of a life well-lived, but they don't have to define you. At Simply You, Nure Aesthetics we take a precision-based approach to neurotoxins, using Botox, Dysport, and Daxxify to refine expression lines, restore smoothness, and preserve youthful balance, while helping your natural beauty shine through

Neurotoxins do more than smooth wrinkles; they are an essential tool in modern aesthetic medicine, used not only for correcting visible signs of aging but also preventing deeper lines from forming in the first place. By relaxing targeted facial muscles, these treatments help maintain a soft, refreshed, and effortlessly youthful appearance.

At Simply You, we offer three of the industry leading neurotoxins: Botox, Dysport, and Daxxify.

Treatment at a glance:


PROCEDURE LENGTH
15-30 minutes, depending on treatment areas.

RECOMMENDED TREATMENTS
Every 3-4 months for Botox and Dysport, and every 4-5 months for Daxxify.

RESULTS
Visible smoothing within 3-7 days, with full results at 10-14 days. Long-term benefits with consistent treatments.

RECOVERY
Minimal to none. Slight redness or swelling at injection sites may last a few hours, with normal activities resumed immediately.

What areas can be treated with Botox?

Forehead Lines & Frown Lines
Smooth the deep creases that form between the brows and across the forehead for a naturally refreshed look.

Crow's Feet
Soften the fine lines around the eyes, restoring a bright and youthful gaze.

Bunny Lines
Minimize crinkling at the bridge of the nose for a smoother, more balanced appearance.

Lip Lines & Gummy Smile Correction
Refine fine lines around the lips and relax the upper lip to reveal a more harmonious smile.

Jawline Contouring & Masseter Reduction
Slim the lower face and ease jaw tension—perfect for achieving a more sculpted silhouette.

Neck Bands (Nefertiti Lift)
Lift and smooth vertical neck lines to enhance the jawline and create a firmer, more elegant profile.

Excessive Sweating (Hyperhidrosis Treatment)
Effectively reduce underarm, palm, or foot perspiration—for long-lasting, confident relief.

Migraines & Tension Headaches
Relax overactive muscles to help alleviate chronic pain and improve daily comfort.
