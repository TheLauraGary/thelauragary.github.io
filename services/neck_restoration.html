Refresh • Hydrate • Smooth


Many of us notice horizontal lines, crepey texture, and early signs of aging on the neck, especially those “tech neck” lines that form from daily posture and screen time. At Simply You, we offer a gentle yet effective Neck Restoration treatment designed to plump, hydrate, and soften visible lines, giving your neck a smoother, more youthful appearance.

What It Treats

Our Neck Restoration service is ideal for

  • Horizontal neck lines (“tech neck”) caused by repeated downward gaze and everyday movement
  • Fine lines and crepey skin
  • Loss of skin hydration and volume
  • Texture irregularities that make the neck look aged or tired

How It Works

We use a skin-hydrating formulation specially chosen to attract and hold moisture deep within the skin, helping to

  • Plump and soften lines for a visibly smoother neck
  • Improve hydration and texture, leaving skin feeling supple
  • Support a natural, refreshed look without dramatic volume changes
    Hydration is a key component of youthful skin — well-hydrated tissues rebound better and appear smoother, especially in areas like the neck where the skin is thinner and more delicate

Each treatment is tailored to your individual anatomy and aesthetic goals. During your consultation, we'll discuss your concerns and craft a plan that complements your overall facial harmony.

Treatment at a glance

  • Procedure Length: 45 minutes
  • Results: Patient will notice improvement in their neck lines starting at 2 weeks and full results at 4 weeks and will last up to 6 months
  • Recovery: Mild swelling and bruising for a few days with normal activities being resumed immediately
