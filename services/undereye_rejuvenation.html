BRIGHTEN & REJUVENATE


At Simply You under eye restoration is approached with the same science-driven precision we apply to aesthetics and regenerative medicine. We use powerful regenerative therapies like PRP and PDGF treatments, to revitalize the under-eye area, reducing dark circles, fine lines, and hollowness.

Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) Therapy

PRP uses your body's own healing factors to stimulate collagen production which can reduce the appearance of dark circles. This treatment works by drawing a small sample of your blood, isolating the platelet-rich portion, and injecting it in your under-eye area.

Platelet Derived Growth Factors (PDGF) Therapy

This under eye rejuvenation treatment uses platelet derived growth factors (PDGF) to focus on stimulating collagen and elastin production for improved skin thickness, firmness, and overall appearance. Unlike PRP, PDGF is a lab-engineered, pharmaceutical-grade version, offering consistent results without the need for a blood draw. It's a next-generation solution, offering up to 300,000 times the results of traditional PRP injections, for addressing under-eye hollows, thinning skin, and dark circles. PDGF offers a more natural and longer-lasting solution than traditional fillers or lasers.
