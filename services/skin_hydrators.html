Deep Hydration • Renewed Texture • Natural Glow

At Simply You, Nurse Aesthetics, our Skin Hydrators treatment is a customized, medical-grade hydration boost designed to rejuvenate skin quality at the foundational level. Unlike traditional dermal fillers that restore volume, Skin Hydrators work beneath the surface to improve hydration, elasticity, and overall skin texture for a smoother, more radiant look that feels fresh and naturally healthy.

What Skin Hydrators Do

Skin Hydrators are advanced hyaluronic-acid-based treatments that:

  • Deeply hydrate dehydrated or dry skin
  • Enhance elasticity, firmness, and suppleness
  • Smooth fine lines caused by dehydration
  • Improve overall skin texture and radiance
  • Support healthier-looking, youthful skin quality without changing your natural contours

Where They Can Be Used

Skin Hydrators are versatile and can be applied to areas including:

  • Radial Cheek Lines: improve fine lines and hydration
  • Neck: to minimize dehydration lines and crepey texture
  • Lower face: to enhance overall texture and hydration

How It Works

Using tiny formulations of hyaluronic acid, a substance that naturally attracts and holds water, we gently deliver hydration beneath the skin's surface where it's needed most. The treatment helps:

  • Pull in and retain moisture, visibly plumping skin
  • Support elasticity and firmness
  • Improve texture and radiance over time

Results are gradual and natural, enhancing your skin's overall condition without dramatic volume changes.

Every Skin Hydrators session at Simply You is tailored to your unique needs, ensuring a comfortable experience and beautiful, natural results.

Treatment at a glance

  • Procedure Length: 45 minutes
  • Results: Patient will notice improvement starting at 2 weeks and full results at 4 weeks that will last up to 6 months
  • Recovery: Mild swelling and bruising for a few days with normal activities being resumed immediately
