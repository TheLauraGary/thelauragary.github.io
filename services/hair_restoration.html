REVIVING HAIR FROM WITHIN


At Simply You hair restoration is approached with the same science-driven precision we apply to aesthetics and regenerative medicine. Our cutting-edge treatments stimulate hair follicles, restore scalp health, and optimize the environment for stronger, thicker, healthier hair growth.

Using a powerful combination of regenerative therapies like PRP or PDGF treatments, along with clinically proven hair supplements, we reignite dormant follicles, improve circulation, and strengthen the foundation of lasting hair health.

Because true hair restoration isn't just about growing hair - it's about creating the ideal conditions for long-term strength, density, and vitality.

Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) Therapy
PRP is one of the most effective non-surgical treatments for hair loss. It uses your body's own healing factors to stimulate dormant hair follicles, increase thickness, and enhance scalp circulation. This treatment works by drawing a small sample of your blood, isolating the platelet-rich portion, and injecting it directly into the scalp.

Platelet Derived Growth Factors (PDGF) Therapy
This hair restoration treatment uses platelet derived growth factors (PDGF) to stimulate dormant hair follicles, increase thickness, and enhance scalp circulation. Unlike PRP, PDGF is a lab-engineered, pharmaceutical-grade version, offering consistent results without the need for a blood draw. It's a next-generation solution, offering up to 300,000 times the results of traditional PRP injections, for addressing hair thinning and loss.

Hair restoration is ideal for those who:

  • Have noticed increased hair shedding or thinning.
  • Have early signs of male or female pattern baldness.
  • Are experiencing postpartum or stress-related hair loss.
  • Want to improve scalp health and slow further hair loss.
  • Prefer a natural, non-surgical approach to hair regrowth.

Treatment at a glance:

  • Mild redness or sensitivity at the treatment site for 24-48 hours.
  • Slight scalp tightness or tingling, indicating increased circulation and follicle activation.
  • Visible improvements in hair texture, thickness, and fullness within 3-6 months.

Since hair naturally grows in cycles, results continue to improve over time. For optimal outcomes, a series of treatments is recommended, followed by annual maintenance.
