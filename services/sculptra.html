This is not about reversing age. This is about embracing it on your terms, with confidence.

What is Sculptra?

Sculptra is not your traditional filler. It is a biostimulatory injectable, meaning that instead of simply adding volume, it triggers your body to rebuild lost collagen over time. Made from poly-L-lactic acid (PLLA), a biodegradable compound that has been safely used in medicine for decades, Sculptra works beneath the surface to awaken your skin’s natural regenerative processes.

Rather than providing instant but temporary volume, Sculptra gradually restores youthful fullness by encouraging the skin to rebuild itself, creating results that evolve naturally and last for years. It does not mask aging - it rewrites the story of your skin from the inside out.

Sculptra is ideal for individuals who want to correct or prevent volume loss without relying on temporary fillers.

It is perfect for:

  • Those experiencing facial hollowing or skin laxity who want a natural, long-term solution.
  • Younger patients looking for collagen preservation to prevent future loss before it happens.
  • Individuals seeking subtle, gradual rejuvenation rather than a dramatic or obvious enhancement.
  • Patients who prefer a biostimulatory approach rather than traditional dermal fillers.

Treatment at a glance:


PROCEDURE LENGTH
30-45 minutes, depending on treatment areas.

RECOMMENDED TREATMENTS
Two to three sessions spaced 4-6 weeks apart for full collagen activation, with results lasting up to two years or more.

RESULTS
Gradual improvement in volume, texture, and firmness over 3-6 months, with a natural lift and contouring effect.

RECOVERY
Minimal downtime. Mild swelling or bruising for a few days, but most patients resume normal activities immediately.

POST TREATMENT

  • Gentle massage of the treated areas for five days to encourage even collagen stimulation.
  • Staying hydrated and maintaining good skincare to support collagen formation.
  • Follow a long-term treatment plan for gradual, natural, and lasting improvements.

With each session, collagen continues to rebuild, creating a foundation of strength, support, and resilience that lasts far beyond the treatment itself.
